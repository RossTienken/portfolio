About Me

Hi, My name is Ross Tienken. I am 21 years old and a Colorado native. I am currently a web developer at Glavanize in Boulder Colorado, and I am working on precfected my craft while I am here.

I first started coding while I was in highschool. I took a class and fell in love with it. I am passionate about CSS, and everything that it allow me to do. I was never artistic while growing up, so nothing ever looked the way that I pictured it. With CSS I can be the artist that I never was, and I am able to make a page look exactly like I originally pictured it.

I hope to one day work for a big company like Google, Facebook, or Snapchat. I find these companies incredibly fascinating, and I want to know more about what goes on behind the scenes at these companies.
